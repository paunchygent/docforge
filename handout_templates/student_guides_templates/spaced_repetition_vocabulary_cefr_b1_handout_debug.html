Spaced Repetition — Vocabulary

Short study. Growing breaks. Say or write the answer before you look.

Session length

5–10 minutes per set. Stop at 10 minutes.

Small rules that help

  • Stop at 10 min; come back later.
  • Say/write the word—don't just recognize it.
  • After tests: still review +1 month & +6 months.

Day 0 (first study)

Quick repeats on the same list:

  • after ~2 minutes
  • after ~10 minutes

Then do these 8 reviews

  • +1 hour
  • Next day
  • +3 days
  • +7 days
  • +16 days
  • +35 days (≈1 month)
  • +90 days (≈3 months)
  • +180 days (≈6 months)

Missed? Do the next review and continue.

How to review (fast routine)

  1. 1) Active recall: hide one side. Say or write the answer before you check.
  2. 2) Mark each word: Hard / Okay / Easy.
  3. 3) Repeat the Hard words 2–3 more times in the same set.
  4. 4) Shuffle the order each time.
  5. 5) Mix old + new: keep 20–30% old words in every set.
  6. 6) Park strong words: if a word is Easy for three sets in a row, leave it until its next due date.

Make your marks change the next date

HARD
Next gap about half as long (e.g., 7 days → 3–4 days).
OKAY
Keep the planned gap.
EASY
Next gap × about 1.5 (e.g., 7 days → 10–11 days).
