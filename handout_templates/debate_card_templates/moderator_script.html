Moderator Script — Team Debates

Scripts, transitions, and timing protocols for running debate rounds smoothly and professionally.

Opening Script

"Good afternoon. This round follows the six-speech format: three speakers per side, two minutes each. During Builder and Closer speeches, the opposing team may offer one 10-second Point of Information. After the closers, we will hold a five-minute POI stage with alternating questions. Judges will score clarity and argument strength, not personal opinion. The motion reads: [insert motion here]. We now begin."

Format & Timing

  • Order: Aff Opener → Neg Opener → Aff Builder → Neg Builder → Aff Closer → Neg Closer
  • Length: 2:00 per speech · POI window: 0:20–1:40 for Builders/Closers · Overtime cut-off: 2:15
  • Signals: 1 bell at 1:40 (POI window closes) · 2 bells at 2:00 (stop) · Interrupt at 2:15

Start timing on the speaker's first word, not when they stand up.

Speech Transition Cues

  1. "The Affirmative Opener will now begin."
  2. "Thank you. The Negative Opener will now present their case."
  3. "Thank you. We move to the Affirmative Builder."
  4. "Thank you. The Negative Builder will now speak."
  5. "Thank you. The Affirmative Closer will now summarize."
  6. "Thank you. The Negative Closer will conclude the debate."
  7. "Thank you. With the Affirmative Closer concluding the six-speech stage, we now move to the final POIs stage. The first POI request will come from the affirmative team."

Closing Script

"Thank you to both teams. Judges, please finalize your scores and prepare to announce your decision. This debate is now adjourned."

Conduct & Troubleshooting

Conduct Interventions (If Needed)

  • Interruption without POI: "Please hold your comments until your next speech."
  • Personal attacks: "Please address the argument, not the person."
  • Excessive overtime: "Thank you, speaker. We must move to the next speech."
  • POI outside window: Quietly signal "not allowed" with hand gesture.

Maintain a neutral, professional tone. Your role is to facilitate, not to judge content.

Common Situations

  • Speaker hesitates to start: Wait 5 seconds, then say "You may begin when ready."
  • Timer fails: Announce "We will restart this speech" and reset.
  • Judge has a question mid-round: "Judges, please hold questions until after all speeches conclude."
  • Team requests clarification: Briefly restate the motion or format rule, then proceed.

Available Debate Motions

  1. Survival Cannibalism: Should Swedish law excuse consumption of human remains in extreme survival situations where no killing occurs?
  2. Death Penalty: Should the state have the power to impose the death penalty for the most serious crimes?
  3. Gene Editing: Should Sweden allow parents to use gene editing to prevent serious diseases in embryos?
  4. Healthcare Rationing: Should hospitals prioritize younger patients over older patients for life-saving treatment during a severe shortage?
  5. Organ Donation: Should all adults be treated as organ donors at death unless they have opted out, with no family veto?
  6. Abortion: Should the law prioritize a person's right to choose over the potential life of a fetus?
  7. Citizenship Tests: Should Sweden add mandatory Swedish and civics tests and a pledge of allegiance as conditions for naturalization?
