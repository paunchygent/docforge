Team Debate — Rules & Planning Sheet

Based on The Great Debate (simplified classroom format)

Debate Format (≈22–24 min, 3 speakers per side)

  • Affirmative Opener — 3 min
  • Negative Opener — 3 min
  • Affirmative Builder — 3 min
  • Negative Builder — 3 min
  • Affirmative Closer (includes rebuttal) — 3 min
  • Negative Closer (includes rebuttal) — 3 min

Optional POIs: up to two per team (≤10 seconds each). The speaker may accept or decline.

Speaker Rules (say these out loud)

  • Use the SEAL structure at least once (Statement → Example → Argument → Link).
  • Say at least two connectors (e.g., firstly, moreover, however, in contrast, for example, therefore, because, on the other hand, as a result, to conclude).
  • Stay polite; no interruptions (except brief POIs).
  • Keep within 3:00; stop at the signal.
  • Judging focuses on clarity, evidence/example, structure (connectors & Links), and responsiveness (one rebuttal).
